Maintenance & Services Expansions

We are sorry to announce a temporary stoppage of some site features due to ongoing site maintenance and expansion of services.

At Anidaso, our main goal is to ensure that travelers and hotel and rental car shoppers find the best deals on travel and hospitality products and services. With that in mind, we've been constantly improving on site features and algorithms. The Platform in its current state only has partial functionality, rest assured enough to find you the best prices on hotels and flights.

Flights: Our flights search and booking engines allow for one way, round trip, and multi-city flight searches and bookings of all cabin and traveler types. One of the restrictions placed on the flights search and booking feature, however, is that of the prevention of inclusions of rental cars and hotel rooms during searches. Also, filtering search by airlines has been disabled due to the ongoing maintenance work. Our developers and technical team are working on these features which should incrementally soon start rolling out to enhace the platform for better user experience and satisfaction.

Stays: The hotels search and booking feature, by far has most of its parts completed. The main search and booking engine for hotel rooms has been fully integrated to facilite your search and booking of hotel room. In its current state, you can find hotel rooms offers from various hotel brands world-wide. You will also, as part of the booking process, be able to see rooms and rates for your chosen hotel brand, then finaly be able to get the final price for a chosen room. However, like the flights search and booking feature, it is the additive parts such as inclusion of flights and rental cars during search that isn't allowed at this particular point in time. Also, you will not be able to add multiple places of stay in a single search.

Major features put on hold: Some of the main functions such as, rental cars search and booking, "Book Cheap/Book Direct" read more..., have been put on complete hold due to this ongoing maintenance works. Our promise to you is that, we will soon re-integrate these features as we contineously roll out updates of the Anidaso.com travel platform.

Property Manager (PMS for Hotel Brands): One of Anidaso.com's main features is its built-in Property Management System(PMS) designed for small to mid-sized hotel brands. The inclusion of this PMS pertain to the Book Cheap/Book Direct feature. With our PMS, new hotel brands that need global recognition can add accounts and manage their properties, rooms, and inventory items. This also makes it possible for our team and algorithms to help get their hospitality products and services to customers all around the world. read more... However, the PMS hasn't been released yet, but as part of our contineous integration of updates to the platform, the "Book Cheap/Book Direct" feature and its supporting PMS should be available as per our maintenance and expansion plans.

Anidaso.com apologizes for any inconviniences caused.
